Traditional Croatian Goulash

Clean the meat from the veins if there are some and cut it into smaller pieces, 3 Ã— 3 cm. Marinate the meat in the mustard and spices and let it sit in the refrigerator for one hour Heat one tablespoon of pork fat or vegetable oil in a pot and fry the meat on all sides until it gets browned. Once the meat is cooked, transfer it to a plate and add another tablespoon of fat to the pot Cut the onions very fine, peel the carrots and shred it using a grater.

Ingredients:

  • Beef - 500g
  • Onions - 2 chopped
  • Carrots - 2 chopped
